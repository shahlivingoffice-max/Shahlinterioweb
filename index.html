About Us

Shah Interio – The Force Behind SECUREX

Shah Interio is the proud manufacturer of the SECUREX brand of premium steel furniture. Under the leadership of Brajesh Steel, we specialize in producing high-quality steel almirahs, storage cabinets, and customized metal furniture for B2B clients across Odisha and beyond.

Our strength lies in our advanced manufacturing setup and modern production technology. We use high-precision machines such as Fiber Laser Cutting Machines, CNC Bending Machines, Almirah Printing Machines, and Glossy Finishing Units. These technologies help us achieve superior accuracy, consistent quality, and a premium finish in every product we manufacture.

Every SECUREX product is designed for durability, rust resistance, and modern aesthetics. We use industrial-grade steel combined with precision engineering to ensure long-lasting performance and strong build quality.

With a focus on bulk production, customization, and timely delivery, Shah Interio has become a trusted partner for wholesalers, retailers, and institutional buyers.
